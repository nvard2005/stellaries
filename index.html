About Us

Stellaris is a global leader in the trading of petrochemicals, oil, gas equipment, and industrial chemicals, headquartered in the thriving business hub of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. With over 15 years of industry experience, Stellaris has earned its place as a trusted partner for industries worldwide, offering a comprehensive suite of high-quality products and solutions tailored to meet the needs of a dynamic global market.

Our strategic location in Dubai allows us to harness one of the world's most efficient logistical networks, connecting us seamlessly to key markets across the Middle East, Central Asia, Africa, Europe, and beyond. At Stellaris, our guiding principle is simple yet powerful: "Business Without Borders."
This motto reflects our commitment to delivering critical resources to our clients, regardless of geographical boundaries.
